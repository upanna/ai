About PageBook


Welcome to PageBook! This is a simple social media platform built with a database, created for literature lovers and for purposes related to religion, social issues, and education, without focusing on photos and videos. It was developed by U Zinn Panna (Web Developer) by combining modern and evolving technologies in programming.

You can create text-based posts text only without images and videos., interact with other members, and use our general chatroom. For private conversations, check out the "Message" section!


Our Mission

Although it may not have the capabilities of a powerful social media website like Facebook, it was created based on the system of a web application using modern web technologies and website backend. This creation serves as a resource for those interested in technology, literature enthusiasts, and writers, providing a platform for mutual support and knowledge sharing.
